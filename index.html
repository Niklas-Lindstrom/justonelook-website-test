Welcome to Just One Look

The purpose of our work is to make one simple insight into the root cause of all human mental misery and one simple act of inward looking that effectively eliminates that cause universally available and accessible to all.

Our goal: to rid humanity of the fear of life, one person at a time.

Learn the Method

The Just One Look Method

The Just One Look Method is an extremely simple approach to mental suffering unlike anything else you have ever tried. It is not a visualization, not a meditation, not an affirmation, and not a self-help program. It is a simple act of attention—an inward looking at the sense of being you.

How It Works

The method involves a simple act of inward looking—a direct noticing of the raw sense of "me" that has always been present at the center of your experience, regardless of circumstances. You don't try to analyze, judge, or change what you find—you simply experience that simple sense of being.

This method gives you the means to free yourself of the fear of life and use your own natural intelligence to develop supple and effective control of your mind. It is claimed to rid you of the root cause of your dissatisfaction with life and the painful yearning for peace and fulfillment.

Step 1: Prepare

Sit quietly and relax. Find a comfortable position where you can focus without distraction.

Step 2: Stabilize Attention

Focus your attention successively on sensory experiences like your tongue, feet, or breath to anchor your awareness in the present moment.

Step 3: Look Inward

Gently direct your attention inward, looking for the subtle flavor of what it feels like to be you—the raw sense of "me" at the center of your experience.

Step 4: Simply Notice

Don't analyze or try to change what you find. Simply experience that simple sense of being without judgment.

About the Just One Look Foundation

The Just One Look Foundation was started in 2000 and operates as a public charitable organization under U.S. law. The foundation is managed by Carla Sherman and a board of directors, originally founded by John and Carla Sherman.

The primary activity of the foundation is promoting and distributing the Just One Look Method globally, making this simple yet profound practice available to all people seeking relief from mental suffering and the fear of life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the "Just One Look" method?

The Just One Look Method is a simple practice of turning your attention inward to notice the raw sense of "me" that has always been at the center of your experience. It's not a meditation, visualization, or technique—just a direct noticing of your sense of being.

How long does it take to see results?

Many people report experiencing shifts immediately or within the first few sessions. However, the method works differently for each person. Some notice gradual changes over weeks or months. The key is consistent practice without expectation or attachment to specific outcomes.

Is this method suitable for everyone?

Yes, the Just One Look Method is simple enough for anyone to practice, regardless of age, background, or experience with meditation. It requires no special equipment, beliefs, or preparation—just a willingness to look inward.
