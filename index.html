Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Just One Look Method?

The Just One Look Method is a simple practice of turning your attention inward to notice the raw sense of "me" that has always been at the center of your experience.

How do I use the chat bot instructor?

Click on "Look at Yourself - Try It" to open the interactive chat bot. The instructor will guide you through the method step by step.

How long does it take?

Many people experience shifts immediately. The practice is simple and can be done in just a few minutes.

Is this suitable for everyone?

Yes, the Just One Look Method is simple enough for anyone to practice, regardless of age, background, or experience.

What is Just One Look?

The Just One Look Foundation was started in 2000 to make one simple insight into the root cause of all human mental misery universally available and accessible to all.

The Method

The Just One Look Method is an extremely simple approach to mental suffering. It involves a direct noticing of the raw sense of "me" that has always been present at the center of your experience.

How It Works

The method is not a visualization, not a meditation, and not an affirmation. It is simply a direct noticing of your own essence—a simple act of looking inward that gives you the means to free yourself of the fear of life and use your own natural intelligence to develop control of your mind.

The Goal

Our goal is to rid humanity of the fear of life, one person at a time, making this simple yet profound practice available to all people seeking freedom from mental suffering.
